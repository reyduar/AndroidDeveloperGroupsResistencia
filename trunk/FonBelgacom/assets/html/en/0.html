What is Fon?

Fon is the world's first global WiFi network built by people like you. We think of it as crowdsourced WiFi.

As a member of the Fon community, you agree to share a little bit of your WiFi at home, and get free roaming at Fon Spots worldwide in return. Sharing WiFi with Fon is safe and secure, and you won't even notice when others are connected because Fon only uses a portion of your bandwidth.

Anyone with a broadband connection can join Fon. Just buy a Fonera and connect it to your ADSL/broadband, or activate Fon through one of our partners. Once your Fon Spot has been connected and registered, you're ready to enjoy all the benefits Fon has to offer!.

Those who don't share their broadband through the Fon network can still connect to Fon Spots around the world by purchasing inexpensive access passes.

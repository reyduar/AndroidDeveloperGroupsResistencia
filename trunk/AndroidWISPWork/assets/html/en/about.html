Fon is the world's largest WiFi network, with millions of hotspots in over 100 countries all around the world. Fon is a network built by the people. To join Fon, you can buy a Fon WiFi device online and connect it at your home or business. Or, you may receive access to the Fon network automatically, simply by being a subscriber to one of our fixed or mobile telco partners. Alternatively, you can buy WiFi passes at any of our hotspots.
